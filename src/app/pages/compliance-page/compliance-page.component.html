Current agreement status

EU-India FTA negotiations are concluded, but legal finalization still matters.

The European Commission states that FTA negotiations concluded on 27 January 2026. The published text is informational, may still be legally revised, and becomes binding only after signature and each side completes the required internal procedures. We use that status as a planning signal, not as a shortcut around compliance.
